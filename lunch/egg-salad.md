# Egg Salad Sandwich

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 12 min | **Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 6 large eggs
- 3 tbsp mayo
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp diced celery
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Paprika (optional garnish)
- Bread or croissants for serving

## Instructions

1. Place eggs in a saucepan, cover with cold water by 1 inch
2. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat, cover, and let sit 12 minutes
3. Transfer to ice water for 5 minutes, then peel
4. Chop eggs and place in a bowl
5. Add mayo, mustard, celery, lemon juice, salt, and pepper
6. Mash and mix to desired consistency
7. Serve on bread or croissants, sprinkle with paprika

## Notes

- Ice bath makes peeling much easier
- Keeps in the fridge for 3-4 days
