# Poultry Brine

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 0 min | **Servings:** 1 batch

## Ingredients

- 1000 g water
- 50 g salt (kosher or pickling)
- 25 g brown sugar
- Herbs of choice (optional, skip for hot wings)

## Instructions

1. Combine water, salt, and brown sugar in a container and stir until dissolved
2. Add herbs if using
3. Submerge poultry in brine and refrigerate

## Notes

- Ratio is 1000:50:25 (water:salt:sugar), multiply as needed for more brine
- Skip herbs if brining for hot wings
