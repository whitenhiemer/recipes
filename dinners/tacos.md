# Ground Beef Tacos

**Prep Time:** 10 min | **Cook Time:** 15 min | **Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup water
- 8 taco shells (hard or soft)

### Toppings

- Shredded lettuce
- Diced tomato
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Sour cream
- Salsa
- Diced onion
- Cilantro
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, drain excess fat
2. Add all spices and water, stir to combine
3. Simmer for 5 minutes until the liquid has mostly reduced
4. Warm taco shells according to package directions
5. Fill shells with meat and desired toppings

## Notes

- This seasoning blend replaces store-bought taco packets
- Double the seasoning and keep it in a jar for next time
