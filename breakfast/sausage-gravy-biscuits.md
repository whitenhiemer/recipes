# Sausage Gravy and Biscuits

**Prep Time:** 5 min | **Cook Time:** 20 min | **Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Gravy

- 1 lb breakfast sausage
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 1/2 cups whole milk
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Pinch of cayenne (optional)

### Biscuits

- 1 can refrigerated biscuits (or homemade)

## Instructions

1. Bake biscuits according to package directions
2. Brown sausage in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking into crumbles
3. Reduce heat to medium, sprinkle flour over sausage and stir for 1 minute
4. Slowly pour in milk while stirring constantly
5. Cook until gravy thickens, about 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently
6. Season generously with black pepper and salt to taste
7. Split biscuits and ladle gravy over the top

## Notes

- Use whole milk for the richest gravy
- Gravy will thicken as it cools, thin with a splash of milk if needed
