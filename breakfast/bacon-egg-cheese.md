# Bacon Egg and Cheese Sandwich

**Prep Time:** 2 min | **Cook Time:** 10 min | **Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- 2 slices bread or 1 english muffin
- 2 strips bacon
- 1 egg
- 1 slice american or cheddar cheese
- Butter for the pan
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy, set aside on a paper towel
2. Pour off most of the bacon grease, add a small pat of butter
3. Crack egg into the skillet, season with salt and pepper
4. Cook to desired doneness -- over easy or over medium works best
5. Toast the bread or english muffin
6. Layer cheese, egg, and bacon on the bread
7. Close and serve immediately

## Notes

- Break the yolk for easier eating on the go
- Add hot sauce or ketchup to taste
